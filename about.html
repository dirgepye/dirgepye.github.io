About

Hi! My name is Shawn! I'm a web developer based out of Montreal with a focus on front-end development. After years of working in either customer service, or as a writer/editor, I decided to make the jump to a career that would challenge my creativity and pique my interest in puzzles and problem solving.

Whether it's developing my own design, or helping bring someone else's vision to life, I love to be part of the creative process. If you want a taste of what I can do, take a look at my Portfolio and then venture over to my Contact page and let me know what you think!
